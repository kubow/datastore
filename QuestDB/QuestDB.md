# QuestDB

High-performance open-source time-series database focused on fast ingestion and SQL analytics.

https://questdb.io/

## Position

- Time-series analytical database.
- Compare with [InfluxDB](../InfluxDB/InfluxDB.md), [Prometheus](../Prometheus/Prometheus.md), [TimescaleDB](../TimescaleDB/TimescaleDB.md), and [TDengine](../TDEngine/TDEngine.md).

## Notes

- SQL interface with time-series functions.
- Designed for high ingestion rates and low-latency analytical queries.
- Common in market data, observability, IoT, and event-stream analytics.
