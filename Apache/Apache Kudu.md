# Apache Kudu

Distributed columnar storage engine designed for fast analytics on mutable data.

https://kudu.apache.org/

## Position

- Analytical storage engine in the Hadoop ecosystem.
- Compare with [Apache HBase](./Apache%20HBase.md), [Apache Hive](./Apache%20Hive.md), [Apache Iceberg](./Apache%20Iceberg.md), [ClickHouse](../ClickHouse/ClickHouse.md), and [Druid](./Apache%20Druid.md).

## Notes

- Designed for fast scans and efficient updates.
- Commonly queried with Impala and other Hadoop ecosystem tools.
- Fits workloads between immutable file-based lakes and row-oriented operational stores.
