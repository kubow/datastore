# Fivetran

Managed automated data movement platform for ELT pipelines.

https://www.fivetran.com/

## Position

- Commercial managed data integration frontliner.
- Compare with [Airbyte](../Airbyte/Airbyte.md), [Estuary](../Estuary.md), [Meltano](../meltano.md), [dlt](../dlt.md), and [Qlik Data Integration](../Qlik/Qlik%20Data%20Integration.md).

## Notes

- Focuses on managed connectors, schema drift handling, and reliable incremental sync.
- Common targets include Snowflake, BigQuery, Redshift, Databricks, PostgreSQL, and other warehouses/lakehouses.
- Often paired with [dbt](../dbt.md) for transformations after loading.
