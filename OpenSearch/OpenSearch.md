# OpenSearch

Open-source search and analytics suite derived from Elasticsearch and Kibana.

https://opensearch.org/

## Position

- Search and log analytics platform.
- Compare with [Elasticsearch](../Elasticsearch/Elasticsearch.md), [Apache Solr](../Apache/Apache%20Solr.md), and vector-search capable databases.

## Notes

- Used for full-text search, observability, security analytics, and log analytics.
- Supports distributed indexing, query, dashboards, and plugins.
- Often appears as the open-source alternative in Elasticsearch-style stacks.
