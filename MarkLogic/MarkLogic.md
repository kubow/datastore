# MarkLogic

Multi-model database for document, semantic, search, and operational data workloads.

https://www.progress.com/marklogic

## Position

- Enterprise multi-model NoSQL database.
- Compare with [MongoDB](../MongoDB/MongoDB.md), [Couchbase](../Couchbase/Couchbase.md), [Neo4j](../Neo4J/Neo4J.md), [Elasticsearch](../Elasticsearch/Elasticsearch.md), and native XML/JSON database notes.

- Strong historical footprint in XML, JSON, search, metadata, and semantic workloads.
- Often used where documents, triples, search, and governance need to live together.
