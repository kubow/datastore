# Prefect

Workflow orchestration platform for data pipelines and scheduled operational tasks.

https://www.prefect.io/

## Position

- Data workflow orchestrator.
- Compare with [Apache Airflow](../Apache/Apache%20Airflow.md), [Dagster](../dagster.md), [Kestra](../Kestra/kestra.md), and [Temporal](../Temporal/Temporal.md).

## Notes

- Python-first flow/task model.
- Handles scheduling, retries, observability, deployments, and orchestration state.
- Good fit for data engineering teams that want code-native orchestration with managed or self-hosted control plane options.
